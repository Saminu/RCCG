Core Values

New Life Assembly is a parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), a Bible believing Church; we believe in the inerrancy of the word of God and that “All Scripture is breathed out by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness” (2 Timothy 3:16). We fellowship with believers from all races, backgrounds and ages, and encourage the congregation and new members in their walk with the Lord.

The Pastor, Pastoral team and the entire Church welcome you as you worship with us for the first time.

As a first time visitor to the Church, we know you are either passing through or will probably stay and become a member of the Church; either way we cherish you as a member of the body of Christ and do believe that the Lord God will keep you and guide you through your walk with Him.

The Church is a praying Church and we believe that as Christians we need to pray always and not to get weary, hence the theme for the Church – House of prayer for all nations (Isaiah 56:7); lf you do not already go to a bible believing church, you are welcome to worship the Lord with us.

Vision

Grounding members in biblical principles that will empower them to express their gifting and creativity.

IN LINE WITH THIS VISION OF SOUND BIBLICAL TEACHING AND GROUNDING, THERE SHOULD BE A FREE-FLOW OF REGULAR FORUMS IN THE FORM OF WORKSHOPS AND EXHIBITIONS WHERE PEOPLE ARE ALLOWED TO EXPRESS THEIR GOD-GIVEN GIFTING AND CREATIVITY; ALONG WITH HELPING TO DEVELOP OTHERS.
